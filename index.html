Our Services

What We Provide

  • Root Canal

    We use the latest techniques to make root canal treatment as comfortable and stress-free as possible, relieving your pain quickly.

  • Alignment Teeth

    From traditional braces to clear aligners, we offer a range of orthodontic solutions to straighten your smile discreetly and effectively.

  • Cosmetic Teeth

    Boost your confidence with our cosmetic dentistry services, including professional whitening, veneers, and complete smile redesigns.

  • Oral Hygiene

    Preventive care is the foundation of a healthy smile. Our hygienists provide thorough cleanings and personalised advice to keep your teeth at their best.

  • Live Advisory

    Have a dental concern and not sure where to start? Connect with one of our dentists online for real-time guidance and personalised recommendations.

  • Cavity Inspection

    Early detection saves teeth. Our thorough cavity inspections use digital X-rays and gentle probing to catch problems before they become costly.

About Us

We Care For Your Dental Health

At Dentassist, we believe everyone deserves a healthy, confident smile. Founded by a team of passionate dental professionals, our clinic combines cutting-edge technology with a warm, patient-first approach to deliver care that truly makes a difference.

Whether you're coming in for a routine checkup or a complete smile transformation, our experienced team is here to guide you every step of the way. We take the time to understand your needs, explain your options clearly, and create a treatment plan that fits your life and your budget.
